Follow the rules

List of all Minecraft and Discord server rules.

Ignorance of the rules could be a ban!

Minecraft server

  1. No hacking, cheating, or using disallowed clients.
  2. No "fake hacking" (e.g., pretending to hack to appear as if using kill aura).
  3. No teaming with hackers.
  4. No using bots for any purpose.
  5. No unapproved mods (check allowed mods list).
  6. No using alt accounts to exploit daily items, gear, or rewards.
  7. No glitch abuse/exploitation (report any glitches immediately).
  8. No abusing /report for false claims.
  9. No inappropriate usernames or skins (includes offensive terms or nudity).
  10. No unfair advantage programs (e.g., autoclickers, macros).
  11. Butterfly clicking is discouraged due to similarities with autoclicking.
  12. No double-clicking or using multiple mice/trackpads to attack.
  13. Drag, bolt, and bawl clicking are prohibited.
  14. Any unlisted clicking methods are assumed to be disallowed.

Discord server

  1. No spamming of any kind (this includes links, mentions, commands, text, etc.)
  2. No inappropriate profile pictures (containing nudity, promoting racism, extremely vulgar, etc.)
  3. No intentionally flooding chat with large copy-pastes or characters - No advertising other Discord or Minecraft servers (This includes "DM me for an invite to...")
  4. Do not evade your mute or ban (e.g., using a new account or changing your nickname to communicate with others)
  5. Do not unnecessarily mention members, including ghost pinging/li>
  6. No solicitation (e.g., "selling/offering Minecraft accounts/capes")
  7. No inappropriate topics (NSFW or anything considerably offensive)
  8. Do not excessively swear
  9. Do not use inappropriate or offensive nicknames
  10. Any form of toxicity (racism, disrespect towards members, slurs, etc.) is not allowed
  11. No suicide encouragement, death wishes, or death threats (includes phrases encouraging self-harm)
  12. Any form of threats (DDoS/Dox threats, death threats, etc.), even if used in a joking manner, will not be tolerated

If you have read the rules, you can go and enjoy the fun on our server!
